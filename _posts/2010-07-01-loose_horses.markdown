title: "Loose Horses"
author: Adam Halbur
---

Busting through a 30 below wind-chill,  
they stretch the distance,  
hooves throwing snow, nostrils  
flaring, manes shaking loose. Until  
at the abandoned road, they brake:  
fences nowhere.
